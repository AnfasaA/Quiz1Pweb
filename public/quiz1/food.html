FOOD!

When you visit new places, local cuisine is one of the most important things you should try. Almost every city in Indonesia has its own local food characteristics, while Batam has seafood as its trademark because Batam is an island surrounded by the sea. So I will show you the foods you must try when visiting Batam, enjoy!

Gong Gong

You may not have heard of gong gong before, the gong gong is an edible sea snail that can be found in the waters of south china. The gong gong has edible flesh inside its shell, people usually take the flesh out using a toothpick, but be careful with its sharp legs. They go well with ice-cold beer, but if you want to drink the gong gong with a beer, make sure you are old enough.

Fish Soup

fish soup is made of tenggiri (wahoo) fish and is well-known nationwide. Despite having branches in other cities, the restaurant at Batam is still the favorite one because of its fresh taste. Aside from fish, the soup typically consists of shrimp and squid. The tenggiri fish head soup, in particular, is cooked with tamarind. The sour taste dispels the fishy smell of the soup. Fish soup is better be eaten when the raindrops or for dinner because it can make your body warm.

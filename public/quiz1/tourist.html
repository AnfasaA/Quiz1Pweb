TOURIST DESTINATION

Because Batam located near Singapore and Malaysia, there’s so many tourist come to Batam to spend their holidays. I’ll show you some places you must visit when you come to Batam, enjoy!

Bintan Island

Bintan Island, which is also known as Negeri Segantang Lada, is an island situated in the Riau Archipelago, Indonesia. Bintan Island also holds some of the important historical sites and tourists prefer visiting them during their stay at the island. You might not find sprawling shopping malls on this island, but you will come across exquisite Indonesian handicrafts like hand-drawn batiks, cultural artefacts, and souvenirs. The island is a great weekend getaway where you can find luxury at an affordable rate along with a lively nightlife.

With a rich history dating back to the early 3rd century, the island is known to have flourished as a trading route between India and China. You can also visit the local villages and find some local dining options and interact with some Malay, Bugis, and Chinese tribes. Besides this, you can also indulge in several water activities like canoeing, jet skiing, surfing, and wakeboarding.

Maha Vihara Duta Maitreya Temple

Maha Vihara Duta Maitreya Temple is the biggest Buddhist temple in South-East Asia, this temple is a peaceful homage to the Buddha Maitreya. The Maha Vihara Duta Maitreya temple is home to some of the largest statues of the many forms of Buddha, primarily the Buddha Maitreya. The temple itself has five sections, with prayer halls and rooms filled with art and sculpture. Tourists wanting to experience another religion can go to this picturesque temple. There is also a vegetarian restaurant within the premises, in addition to a souvenir shop and a clinic. Tourists may take photos within the vihara, which is usually crowded on weekends and holidays.

The power and beauty of the temple draws many people towards it, both locals as well as foreigners. However, the temple offers many rooms which can accommodate a lot of people who wish to offer prayers or meditate. Thus, Maha Vihara Duta Maitreya Temple marks one of the most tranquil and peaceful places to visit on the island. Accompanying the main Buddha statue are 12 other smaller statues which represent the animals of the Chinese zodiac signs.
